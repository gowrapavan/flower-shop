## The Ultimate Divine Offering
The Lotus (*Kamalam*) is not just a flower; it is a symbol of spiritual enlightenment. Rooted in mud but rising above the water to bloom pure and unstained, it represents the detachment required for spiritual growth. It is the favorite seat of Goddess Lakshmi (Wealth) and Goddess Saraswati (Knowledge).

## When to Use?
* **Friday Pooja:** Offering a fresh pink lotus to Goddess Lakshmi on Fridays is believed to bring immense financial stability and abundance.
* **Diwali Lakshmi Pujan:** The most auspicious flower for the Festival of Lights.
* **Navagraha Shanti:** Used in rituals to appease the Sun God (Surya).
