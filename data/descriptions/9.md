# Yellow & Orange Marigold Strings – The Heartbeat of Indian Celebrations

## Transform Any Venue Instantly
When you see Marigolds (*Genda Phool*), you know a celebration is happening. These vibrant strings are the most cost-effective and impactful way to transform a plain venue into a festive wonderland.

## Why Our Strings are Better
* **Densely Packed:** We don't skimp on flowers. Our strings are packed tight, meaning you see flowers, not the thread.
* **Vibrant Mix:** We use a strategic mix of golden yellow and deep orange to create a visual depth that looks rich and full.
* **Long-Lasting:** Marigolds are the warriors of the flower world. They can stay fresh without water for 24-48 hours, making them perfect for outdoor events, sunny days, and multi-day functions.

## Decor Ideas
* **Photo Backdrops:** Hang strings vertically close together to create a solid wall of flowers—the perfect selfie spot!
* **Tree Wrapping:** Wrap them around tree trunks or pillars for a magical garden vibe.
* **Haldi Decor:** The quintessential flower for the Haldi ceremony, symbolizing the glow of the sun.
